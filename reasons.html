Reasons to Own a Reptile

Rarely Make Noise

Pets such as dogs, cats, and even birds can get pretty noisy when you are home, but reptiles make very little to no noise which means they are very unlikely to disturb while you are focused on something else.

Low Maintenance

Obviously your pet reptiles still need to be cared for and given their necessities. However, they are generally easier to care for and require less attention compared to other pets.

No Worries for Damages

Since your reptiles will mostly be in their habitats, there is no worry for any potential damage for things such as furniture, clothing, walls, and etc. Another plus is that reptiles don't have fur so you don't need to constantly clean areas your pets have made contact with.

No Training Required

Pets such as dogs often need to be trained in order to make them obedient and learn to be good pets. Reptiles however, have no need to learn much as they will stay in their enclosures. This means that they don't have to be sent to a daycare or be professionally trained.
